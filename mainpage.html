Intorduction

Romania is situated at the intersection of Central, Eastern, and Southeastern Europe. It has land borders with Bulgaria in the south, Ukraine in the north, Hungary in the west, Serbia in the southwest, and Moldova in the east, as well as a Black Sea entrance. There are 8 regions surrounding it which include Maramures, Bucovina, Crisana, Transylvania, Moldova, Bamat, Walchia, and lastly Dobrogea.

​ It has a climate that is mostly temperate and continental.It has moderate characteristics, as is typical of Central Europe. Summers are scorching, winters are lengthy and severe, and the seasons are distinct. From December through mid-March, Romania may see heavy snowfalls, especially in the higher mountain areas.

Did You Know?

​Romania is the twelfth-largest country in Europe and the sixth-most populous member state of the European Union, with a total area of 238,397 square kilometres (92,046 square miles) and a population of around 19.2 million people (as of 2020).
